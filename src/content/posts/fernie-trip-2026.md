Another trip with the Nikon Z5II + 24-120mm f/4 S combo. 
{{< /lead >}}

During spring break 2026, I spent a week skiing in Fernie, BC -- here are some of my favourite shots from the trip.

nit:
Most of the shots here (except the portrait one, taken at f/4) were taken at f/8, ISO 100, with auto shutter speed on Aperture mode. I think the first one ("On the road") was on Manual mode because we were moving with the car at high speed.
